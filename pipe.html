ACOUSTIC-OPTIC PARK (2017)

 

Made for Lumolab (Shanghai)

Exhibited in Shanghai Popular Science Products Expo - “Technology makes our life better”

 

 

 

Introduction:

 

“Acoustic-optic Park”is an interactive installation consisting of two parts. The transparent tubes on the wall would trigger sound-light interactions: when a visitor shouts into the tube, the length of the LED light would adjust according to the volume of sound. The pipes above the visitors’heads create height-sound interactions: the pipes play different sounds, the frequencies of which change according to the height of the visitors.

 

 

 

Concept:

 

It was designed as a product for indoor entertainment, can be installed in shopping centers for people to get relaxed from busy life. It’s playful for children, while adults could also shout out the stress in exchange for a wonderful audio-visual enjoyment.
