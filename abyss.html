Abyss (2018)

 

Introduction:

 

“Abyss” is my response to several incidents happened in China in these two years, especially in its cyberspace. Information has been deleted, accounts got blocked, every move is under surveillance. Based on the idea of censorship, I designed an interactive installation to bring what happened on the Internet to the physical world, creating a tensive situation for people to experience the real power and violence and to think about their role in such environment. As a result, the art object becomes a game and a collective destructive action.
